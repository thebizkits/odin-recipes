Zombie Antidote

What Is It For

This potion is perfect for zombie apocalypses. The recipe calls for simple, easy-to-find ingredients. However, the procedure can be daunting. It's highly recommended to find a dark, damp place where you can't be disturbed at least 36 hours.

Ingredients

  • Half a gallon of spoiled dairy, full cream
  • Handful of dry soil
  • Eleven strands of hair
  • Half a cup of diesel
  • Around an ounce of infected brain

Steps

Separate the milk curdle and the liquid. In this recipe, we will only need the liquid. Marinade the brain for exactly 7 hours and 7 minutes. Add soil, do not mix. Marinade for another 7 hours and 7 minutes.

While waiting, Ground up the hair strands until its fine. Mix the hair dust and diesel. Let sit.

After 18 hours, put the mixtures in a container. Shake it a few times and let settle for 2 hours. Discard solids, and keep the liquid in clean containers.

When bitten, take a mouthful, gargle for at least 30 seconds, then swallow. Repeat thrice a day for three days.
